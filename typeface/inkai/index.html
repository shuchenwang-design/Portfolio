INKAI marries Chinese traditional calligraphy especially Kaishu (regular script) with Latin typography. It has sharp edges and powerful vision, creating a harmonious balance between traditional Chinese calligraphy and modern typographic principles. Designed for optimal readability, INKAI delivers both cultural richness and practical functionality.

Write letters under the logic of Chinese traditional calligraphy.
