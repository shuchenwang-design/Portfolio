Film Industry

Drew inspiration from classic symbols in cinematic history: the geometry of film reels, the conical beam of prism projection, the cylindrical form of the Zoetrope, and the elegant curves of the Golden Goblet Award.

Festival Spirit

To create a celebratory mood, we incorporated elements like amusement park carousels, floating airships and hot air balloons, glowing sky lanterns, and fireworks displays.

Futurism

The structure is designed like a digital Hanging Gardens of Babylon. It is an indefinable form intended to spark boundless imagination and unrestrained fantasy.

"The reconstruction of a perfect illusion of the outside world in sound, color, and relief...identifies the idea of cinema with that of a total and complete representation of reality."

André Bazin, Qu' Est-Ce Que Le Cinema
