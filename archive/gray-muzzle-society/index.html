OVERVIEW

The Gray Muzzle Society is a non-profit dedicated to giving senior, special needs, and "misfit" animals a second chance. Through foster networks, farm sanctuaries, and reptile rescue programs, they fulfill a touching mission: ensuring every animal grows old in a loving home. The visual identity centers on the texture and flow of animal fur, used as a metaphor for the organization’s impact.

COLOR PALATTE
Inspired by the comforting texture of animal fur, the palette is designed to feel organic and embracing. Tangerine and Burnt Orange mimic the warmth of a loving home, driving the brand's enthusiastic approach to adoption. Complementing this energy are Moss Green and Iris Purple, selected to represent the quiet dignity and hope of senior animals. These colors are utilized as spectrum gradients rather than flat swatches, creating a sense of dimension and soft guidance similar to the natural variation in an animal’s coat.

LOGO
To continue with the concept focusing on the warmth and cohesion of furs and the gray muzzle of senior pets, the logo uses fur-like curves to form letters "G", "M", and "S" as well as showing a dog raising its muzzle.
